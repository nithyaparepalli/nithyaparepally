Visual Presentation of User Story

TCS is developing a product for the logistics industry to provide assurance and advanced visibility to the customer by making every shipment intelligent and self-orchestrated.

I was approached to design a logo for the product, that symbolizes the shipment intelligence and service throughout the world. The final logo references a number of different attributes related to the business, which include:A globe and package to indicate the service throughout the world; a brain to indicate the shipment intelligence. Also, I seized the chance to illustrate user stories graphically.
