Children Book Design

A book illustration and design to retell the story of "Mixed-up Chameleon" by Eric Carle.

Design:  Book layout and Illustration
Tools:  Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign
Year:  2020

Overview

As a part of Text and Image studio, the task was to design a children's book and to work on the illustrations and book layout.

The Problem

The task was to select a children's story and to design a book either using illustrations or photographs. And the size of the book to be 10X10 with a time constraint as 3 days.

Challenge

The challenge was to pick the children's age group and the story that would interest them with the illustrations. And to limit the pages and composition of the layout, to let the children finish the book with interest.

Goal

There were majorly 3 goals for the book design:

  • The illustrations to be colorful and minimal
  • The typography to be understood to the children
  • To explore the colour palettes

    • Outcome

    The illustration and book layout was appreciated by the facilitator, as there was a significant progression in the illustration technique.
