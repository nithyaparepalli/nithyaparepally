Agnee & Dhuan

Comic Strip

ThThe fire is an element that creates a calm and thoughtful vibe. When I stare at the fire and flames, they seem hypnotic and curious. I behold the beauty of lord, illumined by the aarthi flames. I endure the joy, looking at the Diwali festival flames. And, it's unfortunate that the same fire can cause a feeling of grief when recalling the loved ones criminated. But then, I feel astounding the way a flame banishes the darkness and brings hope.

The comic "Agnee & Dhuan" illustrates the idea of the co-existence and co-relation of the two elements fire and smoke, with the latter expressing the different moods and feelings of the former in a biomorphic form.
