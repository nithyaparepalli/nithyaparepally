International Women's Day

Poster Competition
Conducted by Tata Consultancy Services

Tata Consultancy Services conducted an e-Poster competition to celebrate International Women's Day 2018 on the theme 'Gender Parity' from TCS Fit4life perspective.

A bare-breasted woman who appears on the right side holds a baby magnificently, her body posture in a yoga asana illustrating the strength, flexibility, and mind-body awareness as the significance of yoga. A muscular man on the left side exercising represents cardiovascular health and formed muscles. The figure is an allegory of 'ardhanarishvara' - symbolizes the optimal balance of the male and female energies in this world.

I take this opportunity to amplify women and men to drive healthy together and equally. I was awarded 'Best Poster Design of the year 2018'.
